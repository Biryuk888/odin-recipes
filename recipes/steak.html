Steak

Description

A perfectly cooked steak is seared to a golden-brown crust on the outside while remaining tender and juicy on the inside. Rich, savory flavors melt in your mouth with every bite, enhanced by a hint of smoky char.

Ingridients

  • Steak (for example ribeye)
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Fresh herbs like rosemary or thyme

Steps

  1. Bring to room temperature
  2. Season
  3. Heat the Pan
  4. Sear the Steak
  5. Butter Baste
  6. Check Doneness
  7. Rest
